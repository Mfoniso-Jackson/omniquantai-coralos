# Partnerships

## Partnership Principle

Prioritize technical collaboration over logos.

## Target Partners

- Solana ecosystem
- CoralOS
- EasyA
- Imperial AI Society
- university AI and finance labs
- developer communities
- financial-data providers
- AI infrastructure companies
- cloud and tooling providers

## Partner Value Propositions

Solana:

- proof of agent settlement and machine-native markets

CoralOS:

- real multi-agent coordination use case with economic settlement

Financial-data providers:

- agents need reliable, attributed data sources

Developer communities:

- builders can publish specialist financial agents

University labs:

- research agenda around agent reputation, market design, and financial AI safety

## Partnership Package

- live demo
- architecture one-pager
- integration proposal
- co-authored technical article
- developer challenge
- measurable outcome

## Avoid

- vague ecosystem announcements
- token-first partnerships
- integrations that do not improve the market loop
