# Design Partner Program

## Goal

Validate OmniQuantAI on real investment-research workflows before broad commercialization.

## Ideal Partners

- independent researchers
- boutique funds
- family offices
- crypto funds
- university finance labs
- quant researchers

## Offer

Design partners get:

- guided setup
- weekly workflow review
- influence over product direction
- early access to agent-market experiments
- private feedback channel

## Requirements

Design partners provide:

- one concrete research workflow
- weekly feedback
- examples of current memo process
- permission to create anonymized learnings
- success/failure criteria

## Pilot Structure

Week 1:

- discovery call
- workflow selection
- data boundary review

Week 2:

- demo using their chosen research question
- review output quality

Week 3:

- iterate on memo and scoring
- collect trust objections

Week 4:

- decide continue, pause, or convert to paid pilot

## Success Metrics

- time saved
- memo usefulness
- evidence quality
- analyst review effort
- willingness to repeat
- willingness to pay

## Outreach Message

```text
I'm building OmniQuantAI, a Financial Intelligence Network where specialist agents compete to produce investment committee research and get paid through programmable settlement.

We're looking for design partners with a real research-prep workflow, starting with public-equity exposure decisions. No trading execution, no financial advice, just structured research and auditability.

Would you be open to a 30-minute workflow interview?
```
