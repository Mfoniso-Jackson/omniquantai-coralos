# Demo Script

## 90-Second Version

1. OmniQuantAI is a Financial Intelligence Network.
2. A buyer agent asks whether to increase Nvidia exposure over the next 3-6 months.
3. Four specialist seller agents bid.
4. The buyer selects best value, not the cheapest bid.
5. The winner delivers an Investment Committee Memo.
6. The buyer verifies the memo.
7. Solana devnet escrow releases payment.

Closing line:

> OmniQuantAI proves that financial intelligence can become a market: specialist agents compete, deliver useful work, and earn through verifiable on-chain settlement.

## 3-Minute Version

### Problem

Investment research is slow, fragmented, and hard to verify.

### Solution

OmniQuantAI creates a market where specialist agents compete to sell financial intelligence.

### Demo

The buyer asks: should our fund increase exposure to Nvidia over the next 3-6 months?

### Market

Market, news, macro, and portfolio-risk agents bid with price, confidence, timing, and reasoning.

### Decision

The buyer selects best value and explains why.

### Settlement

Escrow locks funds on Solana devnet and releases after verified delivery.

### Close

The report matters, but the deeper product is the network that produces, verifies, and settles intelligence.
