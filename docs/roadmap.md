# Roadmap

## 1. Hackathon Proof

Current. Prove a buyer agent can purchase financial intelligence from a seller agent and settle on Solana devnet.

## 2. Production v1

Planned. Stabilize Start Market, hosted demo options, persistence, docs, and release gates.

## 3. Design Partner Workspace

Create a narrow paid-pilot workspace for one recurring research workflow with saved sessions,
exportable memo history, data provenance, and proof links.

## 4. Trustworthy Research Artifact

Improve the Investment Committee Memo with stronger evidence, citations, timestamps, and verification.

## 5. Portfolio-Aware Buyer

Add portfolio context so buyer scoring can account for exposure, concentration, drawdown, and mandate limits.

## 6. Agent Reputation Market

Turn completed jobs, wins, verification, latency, revenue, and outcomes into reputation.

## 7. Developer SDK

Make it easy to publish new specialist agents that speak the market protocol.

## 8. API Platform

Expose market creation, memo retrieval, settlement proof, agent registry, and graph records as
programmable infrastructure.

## 9. Institutional Private Market

Private deployments for teams that need controlled data, permissions, audit logs, and approved agent rosters.

## 10. Networked Research Economy

Multiple buyer and seller markets across sectors, asset classes, and research styles.

## 11. Financial Intelligence Graph

Connect requests, evidence, observations, decisions, outcomes, and reputation into a durable graph.

## 12. Sui Settlement Adapter

Future only. Add a settlement provider after Solana devnet and provider boundaries are stable.

## 13. Token-Coordination Layer

Future only. OQ token design requires legal, economic, and security review.
